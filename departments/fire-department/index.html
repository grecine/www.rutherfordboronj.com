It is the mission of the Rutherford Fire Department to provide safe, efficient and professional fire and rescue services. For more information please see the Borough Clerk or email Recruitment@rutherfordfd.com
The Rutherford Fire Department is an all-volunteer department that protects the Borough's 18,000 residents within an area of 2.4 square miles, consists of five fire companies and operate out of three firehouses strategically located throughout the Borough:
Ames Avenue Firehouse
Truck 1 and Engine 4
40 Ames Avenue

Mortimer Avenue Firehouse
Engine 2 and SSU
400 Mortimer Avenue

West End Firehouse
Engine 3 and Rescue 5
